Enterprise Knowledge Intelligence Platform

Lead AI Engineer

Built a production-grade RAG system that helps business users search internal documentation and get grounded answers with citations.

Challenge Business users needed instant access to internal knowledge across thousands of documents and procedures.
Architecture Document processing, semantic chunking, embeddings, Azure AI Search, hybrid retrieval, reranking, caching, and memory.
Technology Stack Python, FastAPI, Azure OpenAI, Azure AI Search, Redis, PostgreSQL, Docker, Azure Cloud.
Business Impact Reduced retrieval time from minutes to seconds while delivering grounded answers with citations.
  • Role: Lead AI Engineer
